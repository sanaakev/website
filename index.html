About TellMe

Our mission is to help build networks for those who don't have one. Based in Boston, TellMe is a professional networking app featuring a matching process that directly connects underserved students (high school and college) to leading professionals in their field of interest. What makes TellMe unique is its dedicated and willing-to-help professional base, who offer career advice because they actually want to.
